#+title: ipsec

** Keys
Generating key pair with openssl:
#+begin_src 
openssl genpkey -algorithm x25519 -out x25519-priv.pem
openssl pkey -in x25519-priv.pem -pubout -out x25519-pub.pem  
#+end_src
